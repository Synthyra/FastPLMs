ğŸ¯ TRAINING YOUR OWN SIZE:

If you need a different model size:

1. Modify training config: boltzgen/config/train/boltzgen_small.yaml
2. Adjust parameters:
   - token_s, token_z (embedding dimensions)
   - num_blocks in pairformer
   - max_tokens, max_atoms (capacity)
3. Train following BoltzGen instructions
4. Use your checkpoint:
   
   model = load_model(
       checkpoint_path="./workdir/my_custom_size.ckpt",
       device="cuda"
   )
